Miscellaneous Art

When outside the bounds of pixelart, I like to emphasize flowing lines in both my characters and environments, as well as using a lot of warm color pallettes. I use line-thickness to add a sense of playfulness to the details of my work that I think really influence the final pieces. These are some of my favorite non-pixelart works!
